SEATTLE'S FAVORITE LUXURY
HAIR SALON

HAIR MODE由知名发型设计师 Michael Dong（董伟青）创立于 2003 年。至今一直秉承着简约，时尚，优雅，独特的设计理念， 坚持高级定制式品质服务，在纽约的社会名流，艺术家，设计师，白领及年轻族群拥有相当高的知名度。历经多年的造型设计生涯，Michael Dong 对设计理念的执着与坚持令他成功跻身于美国和亚洲顶尖时尚界，成为众多国际大牌合作的风向标。Michael Dong和他的 HAIR MODE 将带你一起体验无与伦比的发型艺术。 HAIRMODE is an exclusive, boutique hair salon located in the heart of Flushing, 11355 and Great Neck, 11021. Lead stylist Michael Dong founded HAIRMODE to bring his vision of an modern, elegant space that redefined the salon experience to life. A favorite of the hair conscious elite, Michael and his team does just that—provide the ultimate upscale experience for our choice clientele. Enter our oasis—modern yet inviting, sophisticated yet welcoming—and indulge in HAIRMODE.
